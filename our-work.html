Our Acts:

Free Sanitary Towels : Ensure girls in rural schools have consistent access to menstrual products to avoid missing class.
Menstrual Health Workshops : Educate girls (and where appropriate, boys and teachers) about periods, hygiene, and breaking taboos.
Provide Personal Hygiene Starter Kits : Include pads, soap, underwear, and information brochures.
Set Up “Sanitary Corners” in Schools : Safe, private spaces for girls to clean up and change with dignity.
Empower Peer Ambassadors : Train older girls as hygiene champions to mentor and support younger girls.

Our Acts:

Hygiene Awareness Days : Teach proper handwashing, dental care, and cleanliness routines through fun, interactive sessions.
Distribute Hygiene Kits : Provide soap, toothpaste, toothbrushes, nail clippers, and combs to boys in need.
Facilitate Self-Worth & Confidence Talks : Use male mentors to speak on hygiene, responsibility, and healthy identity.
Organize Grooming Clinics : Offer free haircuts, basic grooming, and hygiene checks quarterly.
Launch School Hygiene Clubs : Encourage peer-led groups to promote personal hygiene and sanitation.
