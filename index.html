Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about getting involved with our organization.

What do I need to Volunteer?

Our volunteers must be at least 16 years old (or accompanied by an adult), complete our application process, and attend an orientation session. It is open to anyone willing to help

How much of the donations goes directly to the programs?

We're proud that 85% of all donations go directly to our programs and services. The remaining 15% covers essential administrative costs and fundraising efforts that allow us to continue and expand our work.

Can I volunteer as team or group?

Absolutely! We welcome group volunteering from companies, schools, religious organizations, and other community groups. We can accommodate groups of various sizes and can work with you to find a project that matches your interests and availability.

How can my company be involved?

Companies can partner with us through corporate donations, matching gift programs, sponsorships, and employee volunteer opportunities. We also offer custom corporate social responsibility programs tailored to your company's goals and interests.

Can I specify where I want my donation to go?

Yes, you can designate your donation for a specific program or initiative. During the donation process, you'll have the option to select which area you'd like your contribution to support.
